Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular and delicious food that originated in Italy. It is usually made with a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. Pizza can be baked in an oven, and its crispy crust combined with melted cheese and flavorful toppings makes it loved by people all over the world. There are many types of pizza, like Margherita, Pepperoni, and Veggie, each with its own unique taste. Pizza is not only a favorite meal at restaurants but also a fun food to make at home with friends and family

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough – made from flour, water, yeast, and a little salt.
  • Tomato sauce – usually spread over the dough as the base.
  • Cheese – commonly mozzarella, for a gooey topping.
  • Toppings – like vegetables (tomatoes, onions, bell peppers), meats (pepperoni, chicken), or herbs (basil, oregano).

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 220°C (425°F)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a round or rectangle.Place it on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Place your chosen vegetables, meats, or herbs on top of the cheese.
  6. Put it in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Take the pizza out, let it cool slightly, slice it, and enjoy your homemade pizza!

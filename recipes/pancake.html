Pancake

Description

Pancakes are a delicious breakfast food made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a little sugar. They are cooked on a hot pan until golden brown. Pancakes are soft and fluffy, and you can serve them with syrup, fruits, or chocolate spread. They are loved by kids and adults alike because they are easy to make and taste amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Toppings: syrup, fruits, or chocolate spread

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg and milk together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients to make a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a pan and add a little butter or oil.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter on the pan and cook until bubbles appear on top.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.

Pasta

Description

Pasta is a popular Italian dish made from dough of flour and water, sometimes with eggs. It comes in many shapes, like spaghetti, penne, or macaroni. Pasta is usually boiled and served with sauces such as tomato sauce, cheese sauce, or pesto. It is loved worldwide for being simple to cook, filling, and very versatile.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or macaroni)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot with a pinch of salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8–10 minutes until soft.
  3. Drain the pasta and keep it aside.
  4. In a pan, heat olive oil, add garlic, and sauté for a minute.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  7. Serve hot with grated cheese on top.
